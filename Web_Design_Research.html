What Web Design is

A simple explanation of Website Design is the act of designing and creating websites. It is considered to be a form of art.

During the early 1990's, after the introduction of the World Wide Web, there were literally just a handful of websites online. They then grew in number rapidly during the end of the 20th century. There are now millions of websites today.
